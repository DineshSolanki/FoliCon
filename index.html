## Features * Folder Icons(4 different designs) with Rating. * Apply already downloaded ico or png as folder icons. * Chose from multiple poster available for a media. * See trailer/video right in the FoliCon. * Preview poster in high quality before picking. * Give your own rating for media poster. * Available in multiple languages. * Supports Game, Shows, music and Movies/collections/anime Folders. * Professional Mode makes your folder icon professional with publicly available personally designed Folder icons. * Shows Story overview, Year and rating. * Automatic selection of icons minimizes the work user has to do.
